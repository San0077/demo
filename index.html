About Mice

Mice are small rodents that are often kept as pets. They are known for their agility and curious nature.

Types of Mice

  • House Mouse: Commonly found in homes and buildings.
  • Field Mouse: Often found in fields and grassy areas.
  • Pet Mouse: Domesticated mice kept as companions.

Mouse Care

Proper care for pet mice includes providing a suitable cage, a balanced diet, and regular veterinary check-ups.
